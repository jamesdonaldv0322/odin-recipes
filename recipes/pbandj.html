Peanut Butter and Jelly

Peanut butter and jelly, a timeless classic, easy to make. You only need three ingredients!

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly

Steps

  1. Get two pieces of bread
  2. Spread peanut butter on one slice
  3. Spread jelly on one slice
  4. Put the pieces together
